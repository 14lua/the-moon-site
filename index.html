Welcome

I'm glad that you are paying a visit to the site. This is a site that I created for my projects - sort of like a portfolio. Here you'll find a collection of all the things that I am working on, which also allows me to draw some lines between them and introduce people interested by one of my projects to my other works - but also it gives me an excuse to finally work on a personal page.

Who am I?

Theres a good chance you found this site completely by accident, so allow me a few lines to introduce myself to you!
Online, I go by Lua, but if you care about my real name, it's Luan - creative, i know. In my early teens, I found artistry for myself and used it as a medium to let my creative mind let off some steam and put my feelings into pretty pictures when they got overwhelming. Skip a few years - I finally got my hands on the tools to create music, which has been a long time dream of mine by then. By now I have a relased a couple of tracks and working on a bunch more.
Other than that, I like to code and every once in a while work on something new.

The point is...

I see art as a way of connecting people. My motivation to create is equally political/philosophical as it is emotional. In the somewhat little time I spent as a musician, I formed plenty of connections with like-minded people and I wish to create a platform for people to do the same. This is becoming ever more important in the ever more declining condition of the world.
